About Us

Event Sheker is a platform dedicated to empowering women through meaningful cultural exchanges and skill-sharing opportunities. Our mission is to connect successful businesswomen traveling for work with local women's communities in the countries they visit. We organize unique experiences where travelers immerse themselves in local traditions while giving back by sharing their career expertise through workshops and mentorship. Together, we aim to foster connections that inspire mutual growth, promote cultural appreciation, and empower women globally.
